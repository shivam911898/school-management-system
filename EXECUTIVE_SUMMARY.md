## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigation

| Risk                      | Probability | Impact | Mitigation                       |
| ------------------------- | ----------- | ------ | -------------------------------- |
| **Teacher resistance**    | Medium      | High   | Proper training, phased rollout  |
| **Data migration issues** | Low         | Medium | Validation, testing, backup      |
| **User adoption is slow** | High        | Medium | Regular support, incentives      |
| **Scope creep**           | High        | High   | Clear requirements, fixed phases |
| **Bugs in first month**   | High        | Low    | Thorough testing, support team   |

**Mitigation Strategy:**

1. Involve teachers from Day 1
2. Start with one department as pilot
3. Provide 24/7 support in first month
4. Have backup manual processes ready
